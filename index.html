About Me

Hi, I am Hua, Hsuan-Fu. I am currently a Ph.D. student major in statistics. I am now studying in National Chengchi University, Taiwan, where I also got my Master degree. I am advised by Professor Ruby Chiu-Hsing Weng. I received Bachelor degree from Department of Statistics, National Cheng-Kung University, Tainan. In NCKU, I worked as an undergraduate research assistant under Professor Sheng-Mao Chang and Professor Ray-Bing Chens' adviced.

My research interest lies in Bayesian inference. I am particularly interested in Bayesian model selection and Bayesian Optimization. I am also interested in sports statistics and enjoy developing models to help build a better team and search for better game-planning strategies. I have researched in player rankings dynamic modeling under the framework of the Kalman filter and unsupervised learning based on the Gaussian Mixture Model(GMM) performed on World University Rankings.
